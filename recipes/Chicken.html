Chicken and Cheese Burritos

Chicken, Cheese, and Mayo wrapped in Tortillas

To prepare this recipe you first have to put your already precooked chicken onto a cutting board and trip the fat and cartilage from the chicken breast. Then you grate the Cheddar slowly making sure not to have huge chunks. Then you combine with mayo and wrap.

Ingredients

  • 6 oz of pre shredded chicken breast
  • 2 oz of Mild Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Tbsp of Mayo
  • 3 Flour Tortillas

Steps

  1. Remove excess fat from chicken
  2. take cheese and shred evenly
  3. Combine into pan with Mayo
  4. spread evenly on Tortillas and wrap
  5. Enjoy!
