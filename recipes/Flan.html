Flan

Sweet and sugar filled flan

To make flan you need eggs, sugar, milk and condensed milk and evaporated milk. You mix them all together in a bowl and put them in the oven and let cool.

Ingredients

  • half a cup of sugar
  • 1 can of Condensed milk
  • 1 can of Evaporated Milk
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 4 Eggs

Steps

  1. Crack Eggs into bowl and whisk
  2. add whole milk into bowl
  3. add evaporated and condensed milk into bowl
  4. add sugar and whisk all together
  5. Set oven to 375 F and preheat then bake
  6. Let cool until warm then Enjoy!
