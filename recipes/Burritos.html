Egg and Bacon Breakfast Burritos

Three Egg and Bacon burritos that that are wrapped with cheese.

To prepare the recipe you need to get three eggs and crack them, whisking them into a bowl being sure to mix them well. Next you need to cook the bacon on the stove on medium heat to make sure it doesn't burn. Cutt the bacon up and add it into the same pan with the eggs and add the cheese. Then Wrap.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 oz of Cheddar
  • 3 Tortillas
  • 4 Bacon Strips
  • 1 Tbsp of Butter

Steps

  1. Crack Eggs into bowl and Whisk
  2. heat stove to medium and cook bacon
  3. cut up bacon and return to stove
  4. add butter and eggs to stove with bacon
  5. take cooked food and wrap with Tortillas
  6. Enjoy!
